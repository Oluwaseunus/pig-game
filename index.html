Welcome to the pig game, written by Seun Adetunji

The rules of the game are simple:

  1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  2. In each turn, a player rolls two die as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his current score.
  3. BUT, if the player rolls two 1s, all his current score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  4. If a player also rolls two 6s, his current score is added to his total score, and then halved.
  5. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his current score gets added to his total score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  6. The first player to reach 500 points on total score wins the game.
